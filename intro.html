Introducing VGuard®

The VGuard device is dedicated for patients with mild cognitive impairment (MCI), specially for patients with Alzheimer’s disease or dementia above 60 years old. VGuard device will be a pioneering solution on a global scale, introducing a new method of therapy delaying the development or reducing symptoms of the disease. VGuard consists of a headband with a built-in neurostimulator. What is important VGuard device will be working in wireless bluetooth connection to personal mobile devices, what gives great opportunity to monitor treatment compliance.

Technology

Vagal nerve stimulation (VNS) has been used in clinical practice for more than 20 years. In our project we combined best clinical experience comming from that method with innovative approach to stimulation and as a result we target human brain structure involved in memory consolidation in non-invasive way. Those structures are heavily affected by neurodegenerative process of Alzheimer’s disease: hipocampus, amygdala, locus coareleus. By long term animal trial we are able to establish most effective stimulation protocols and those results were confirmed on human in fMRI (functional magnetic resonance imaging). Vagal nerve as a stimulation target was selected per purpose due to its role in maintaining of humans homeostasis. It was already proven in other clinical application that vagal nerve stimulation can be effective tool in treatment of various neuropsychiatric disorders eg. epilepsy, despresion, migraine.
